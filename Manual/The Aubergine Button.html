On the Subject of The Aubergine Button

This is a button. It is aubergine.

The text on the module cycles between three hiragana, of which the screen shows a square slice. Rotate the bomb to move the slice around.

The three hiragana make up a word in the wordlist below with one of the hiragana replaced by another. Press the button when the replaced one is showing to solve the module.

あたまからだせなかこゆびほのお
こおりにほんたまごさらださかな
くじららくだきりんぱんださくら
ひかりぼたんいのちからすこいぬ
